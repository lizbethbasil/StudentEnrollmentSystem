Course Overview

Registration for the next batch starts on 15th Aug 2022.

Machine Learning (ML) and Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming our world and the adaptation rate of ML/AI technologies is high. The products of the new AI revolution, like self-driven vehicles, robot assistance, and the digital disease diagnostics will affect how we live and function. ML/AI has the power to produce some of this century’s most important and revolutionary inventions as well. The demand for qualified engineers has more than doubled in recent years, and professionals who want to take a lead in research and development in AI are providing endless opportunities. Whether you’re a student, a developer, or a technology consultant - understanding ML/AI and knowing how to create real-time applications can give you an edge in your career. This program is designed to provide the learners a firm understanding of ML/AI technologies and its applications.

Course Info

The PGP in Data Science and Machine Learning is offered on two levels.
Level 1: Certified Specialist in Data Science and Analytics.
Level 2: Certified Specialist in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence.

Those who have successfully completed both certifications are eligible for the PGP in Data Science and Machine Learning.
